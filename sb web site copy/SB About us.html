About us

Who we are,

For 40 years, We have been able to go beyond the principle of being just an society and improve the quality of life of the rural people to provide the most reliable service to the rural people.

It registered under the society act and established on 1982 as Sarvodaya Shramadana society.

We have been able to become friendly with the local people by supporting almost any social service activity in the area. Our primary objective is to empower micro, small and medium scale enterprises, which will greatly contribute to the economic development of the country inculcate saving habit in rural people and encourage people to save by offering high interest rates on saving and fixed deposits.

We are very happy to announce that very strong group of sponsors are forming around our society. Who willingly provide labor and financial contributions for the social services and charity activities carried out by the society.

As a result of 40years of dedication and faithfulness of a large body of officers, Sharamadana and welfare society has started a strong journey today.
